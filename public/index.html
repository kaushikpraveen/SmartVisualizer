SmartVisualizer

The NodeJS Backend for SmartVisualizer app is running

SmartVisualizer is an app that allows the user to receive a simple audio description of pictures taken with a smartphone camera. The app is build with Watson services on IBM BLuemix

Try the Android App now

Download the .apk from http://smartvisualizer.mybluemix.net/app/smartvisualizer.apk

After installing the app, open it, turn up the volume of your phone and click on the “Take Picture” button. After taking the picture, the image will be analyzed and you will hear the audio sentence describing it.
